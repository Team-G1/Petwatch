Help & Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I book an appointment for my pet?

Answer:
You can book an appointment by selecting the "Our Services" option on our homepage. Choose your preferred location, service type, and a convenient time slot. Fill in your pet's details and submit the booking.

2. What services does PetWatch offer?

Answer:
PetWatch provides a wide range of services, including pet grooming, veterinary care, and health consultations. You can explore these services under the "Our Services" section on our website.

3. Are your services available across the island?

Answer:
Yes, PetWatch offers islandwide services in five locations. You can choose the one closest to you when booking an appointment.

4. Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

Answer:
Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your appointment through your account dashboard. Simply navigate to your upcoming bookings, and select the option to modify your appointment.

5. How do I access pet health tips?

Answer:
Our "Keep Your Pets Healthy" section offers valuable health tips. You can find articles and advice to help you care for your pet, from grooming techniques to dietary recommendations.

6. Can I edit my pet's details after registering?

Answer:
Yes, you can update your pet's profile by going to your account dashboard and selecting the "My Pets" section. From there, you can edit details like age, breed, and weight.

7. How can I contact customer support?

Answer:
You can reach us by clicking on the "Contact Us" link at the bottom of the website. Alternatively, you can email us at pw@gmail.com or call our hotline for assistance. Or use the Inquiries form below.
